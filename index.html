Why choose Swhen?

Why?

We believe in genuine and simple communication. Communication is the key to start and maintain relationships. We think that waiting for a package should be enjoyable and fun experience

What?

Meet Swhen. Your automated assistant in package tracking. He works like magic - You say hello and Swhen asks for the tracking number and what is the shipment. After that it’s like talking to your best friend. He’ll be with you from the beginning until the end. Simple as that.

How?

Today, people don’t know where their package is nor they have a clue when will it arrive. Thus, we will solve this issue building a tracking bot which gives you inner peace sharing simple stories and notifications about your package.
